Abbott Fay's Books

Tue, 05/15/2007 - 12:20 â€” rfay

Here is a list of Abbott Fay's books. He's been a prolific writer in recent years. All of his recent books have been published by Western Reflections. Many of these are in print and easily available - you can get them at local Colorado bookstores (including the chain stores like Barnes and Noble and Borders) or through Amazon.com or the publisher, Western Reflections.

  • I Never Knew That About Colorado - A Quaint Volume of Forgotten Lore (2001)
  • Beyond the Great Divide (2005)
  • The Story of Colorado Wines (2002)
  • A History of Skiing in Colorado (1999)
  • More That I Never Knew About Colorado
  • Grand Mesa Country: Stories from Mesa & Delta Counties in Colorado (2005)
  • To Think That This Happened in Grand Country (1999)
  • Famous Coloradans: 124 People Who Have Gained Nationwide Fame (1990)
  • Ski Tracks in the Rockies: A Century of Colorado Skiing (1984)
  • Writing Good History Research Papers (1980)
  • Mountain Academia: A History of Western State College of Colorado (1968)
